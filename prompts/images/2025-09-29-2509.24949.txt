> **Prompt:**
> An abstract, artistic representation of gravitational waves being processed by a massively parallel system. ### Option 3 (Metaphorical Breakthrough)

This prompt is a strong metaphor for the paper's core achievement: overcoming a computational bottleneck. It's dynamic and action-oriented.

> **Prompt:**
> A powerful, metaphorical image representing a computational breakthrough. On the left, a single, slow, dim thread of light enters a dark, complex bottleneck. The emergent beam subtly forms the shape of a gravitational wave signal as it speeds across a dark, cosmic background.
