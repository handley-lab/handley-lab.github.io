### Primary Prompt

This prompt attempts to capture the main ideas: a closed (spherical) universe, the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) as the data source, the idea of quantization (discrete levels/steps), and the underlying CPT symmetry (a mirror-image or cyclical nature).

```
An abstract, artistic representation of cosmology. In the center is a glowing sphere, reminiscent of the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) with its intricate patterns of hot and cold spots. The surface of this sphere is not smooth but is structured with faint, concentric, glowing rings or steps, suggesting its curvature is quantized into discrete levels. The entire scene has a profound symmetry, as if the universe is reflected across a central plane, hinting at a CPT-symmetric or cyclical nature. The style is ethereal and cosmic, with nebular colors and glowing light. No text, numbers, or graphs. Digital art, science fiction aesthetic, cinematic lighting.
```

### Breakdown of Key Elements in the Prompt:

*   **"glowing sphere, reminiscent of the Cosmic Microwave Background"**: This grounds the image in the observational data (CMB) and the concept of a closed universe (a sphere).
*   **"not smooth but is structured with faint, concentric, glowing rings or steps"**: This is the visual metaphor for the core idea of **quantized curvature**. It avoids being a literal graph and instead integrates the concept into the geometry of the universe itself.
*   **"profound symmetry, as if the universe is reflected"**: This visually represents the **CPT-symmetric** framework, which is a foundational assumption of the paper.
*   **"Ethereal and cosmic, nebular colors, glowing light"**: These are stylistic instructions to ensure the image is visually appealing and suitable for a headline.
*   **"No text, numbers, or graphs"**: This explicitly follows your instructions.
*   **"Digital art, science fiction aesthetic, cinematic lighting"**: These keywords help guide the AI towards a high-quality, modern, and dramatic final image.
