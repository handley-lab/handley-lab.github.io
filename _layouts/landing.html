Core Framing

Two Revolutions, Two Acceleration Modes

Revolution What it accelerates Examples

AI for Scientists The researcher Reducing overhead, building infrastructure, rapid tooling

Methodology

Foundation Models

Useful for brainstorming, but limited as a complete research workflow.

Autonomous Agents

Can create volume quickly, but quality control and scientific responsibility remain unresolved.

Supervised Execution

Human intent and verification + AI implementation. This is our default operating model.

80/20: the hard 20% stays human (physics, algorithms, judgement); AI collapses the tedious 80%.

Generation / Selection / Propagation: AI is strongest at generation, humans remain responsible for selection.
